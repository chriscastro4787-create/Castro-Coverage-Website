What is an NPN?

A National Producer Number is a unique ID assigned by the National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC) to every licensed insurance producer in the country. It's the standard way to confirm someone is actually licensed — not just claiming to be.

How to verify us

Use the NAIC's State Based Systems license lookup and search NPN 22109263 or "Castro Coverage," or check directly with your own state's Department of Insurance. Both will confirm active licensure.

Why we publish this

Health insurance is too important to take on trust alone. We'd rather you confirm we're who we say we are before we ever discuss your coverage — no exceptions, no shortcuts.
