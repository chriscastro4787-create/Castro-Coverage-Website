For Freelancers & Gig Workers

Health Insurance That Doesn't
Need an Employer

No W-2, no group plan, no HR department — just you, figuring out coverage on your own. We make that part easy.

Freelancing, consulting, rideshare, delivery — the moment you're 1099 instead of W-2, health insurance stops being a checkbox on an onboarding form and becomes something you have to build yourself. Most people either overpay for a plan they don't understand, or go without.

The Self-Employed Coverage Problem

  • No employer contribution, so the full premium is a personal budget line
  • ACA marketplace subsidies depend on income that can swing month to month for gig work
  • Bare-bones "just in case" plans that don't actually cover a routine doctor visit
  • Decision paralysis from too many plan types with no one to compare them side by side

What We Compare For You

  • Private $0 doctor visit PPO plans alongside ACA marketplace options
  • Whether you qualify for subsidies based on your actual income
  • Nationwide PPO access, since freelance work doesn't always mean staying in one metro
  • A predictable monthly cost either way, so budgeting isn't a guessing game
There's no universally "wrong" plan type here — private and marketplace can both work. The point is comparing both instead of guessing.

How It Works

  • Tell us where you are and what matters most — network, budget, prescriptions.
  • We compare private $0 doctor visit PPO plans and marketplace options across 50+ carriers.
  • You pick a plan, we handle enrollment. Zero cost to you, zero pressure.
